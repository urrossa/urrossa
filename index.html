About Swastika Boutique

Our Story

Founded in 2021, Swastika Boutique started as a small artisan workshop creating handcrafted hair accessories. Today, we've expanded to offer a curated collection of boutique products including hair pins, clips, shoes, and socks.

Our mission is to bring elegance and craftsmanship to everyday fashion. Each product is designed with attention to detail, using high-quality materials that ensure both beauty and durability.
